Welcome to the Pig-Game

Instructions

  • There are two players. Each player plays in turn
  • In their turn, a player can roll the dice as many times as they want to.
  • Every time a dice is rolled, the score will be added to their current score
  • If a player rolls 1 on the dice, their current score becomes 0 and their turn is over
  • The player can hold their current score, which gets added to their total score
  • First player to accumulate a total score of 50, wins the game
